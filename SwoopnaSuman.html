Biography

Swoopna Suman is a Nepalese singer, songwriter, and musician known for his soulful voice and heartfelt compositions. Born on October 24, 1994, in Nawalparasi, Nepal, Suman gained popularity through his participation in the Nepali music reality show "Nepal Idol" in 2017. He captivated audiences with his remarkable talent and has since become one of the most beloved artists in the Nepali music industry.

Swoopna Suman discovered his passion for music at a young age. Growing up, he was influenced by various genres, including folk, rock, and pop. He started his musical journey by performing at local events and eventually began uploading cover songs on YouTube, which garnered attention and helped him build a dedicated fanbase.

Swoopna Suman's breakthrough came when he auditioned for the first season of "Nepal Idol" in 2017. His mesmerizing performances and soulful renditions of popular Nepali and international songs earned him immense praise from the judges and viewers alike. Despite finishing as the first runner-up, he won the hearts of millions and solidified his position as a rising star.
